About Noobs Vs Bacons

Noobs Vs Bacons is an action packed, team based Roblox experience where chaos meets strategy!

Release Date: December 18, 2024

Creator: @fadi_fazbar

Why We Created It: After the failure of one of fadi's old games (Laser Tag Gaem) he got tired and eventually he asked the co-owner about noobs vs bacons, fadi had the game idea from a random ahh midnight thought and decided to make it am actual thing!
