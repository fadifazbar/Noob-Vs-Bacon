About Us

Noobs And Bacons is a roblox team based fighting game taking the classic theme of roblox, In here players choose sides between Noobs and Bacons, battling for supremacy. Created by @fadi_fazbar and @iwantlightfruitplz, the game combines strategic combat with dynamic gameplay, encouraging teamwork and rivalry between the factions.

The game was created in 12/18/2024 and has since grown and made a small community of players who embrace the chaotic battle between the two iconic groups. Join the fight, choose your side, and prove your loyalty!
