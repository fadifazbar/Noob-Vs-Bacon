About Noobs Vs Bacons

Noobs Vs Bacons is a fast-paced, team-based Roblox experience where chaos meets strategy.

Released: March 15, 2024

Creator: GameDev_Master

We built this game to celebrate the joy of silly, high-energy teamwork — where being a "Noob" is a badge of honor, and "Bacon" means you’ve got the skills to back it up.
